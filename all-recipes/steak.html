Steak

Description:

Medium rare steak cooked with butter and garlic

Ingredients

  • Preferred cut of steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves of fresh garlic

Steps:

  1. Add olive oil to a pan and wait for it to heat until the pan is smoking
  2. Place steak into pan and press the steak firmly down onto the pan
  3. Cook 1 min on each side and flip the steak
  4. Add butter and full garlic cloves to pan and cook the steak in the butter and garlic for 2 - 3 minutes on each side for a medium rare steak
