Spaghetti

Description:

Spaghetti with meat sauce that is quick to make and extremly tasty

Ingredients:

  • 1 lbs lean ground beef
  • 1 cut chopped onion
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1/2 teaspoons of dried oregano
  • 1 cup of water, broth, or red wine
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 12 ounces of dried spaghetti noodles
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmasan cheese
