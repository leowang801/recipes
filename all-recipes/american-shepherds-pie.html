American Shepherds Pie

Description:

Classic beef american shepherds pie topped with baked mashed potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1 can condensed vegetable soup
  • 1/22 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 3 cups mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and lightly grease a 2 quart baking dish.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, stir and cook ground beef, onion and celery until juices run clear.
    Drain. Pour mixture into baking dish with vegetable soup and thyme; stir well. Spread mashed potatoes on top.
  3. Bake 20 minutes. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top and bake 5 minutes more, or until cheese has melted.
